Reflector

Welcome to Reflector! Reflector is a tower defense game where the towers all shoot lasers. Your goal is to position and aim your lasers in such a way that they reflect off the silver reflectors around the map and destroy your enemies.

Red lasers are your basic lasers. They do medium damage and have an average armor pierce. They also have an average fire rate.

Blue lasers are slower than red lasers and do less damage. However, they slow the enemy for two seconds. Very useful.

Yellow lasers are constant beams. They do more damage than red lasers but have no pierce, making them weak against armored units.

Green lasers are also beams but they have a cooldown period. They don't do as much damage as yellow lasers, but they have high pierce and cause a poison effect. Very useful against heavily armored units.
